# ProofVault

Turn verbal agreements into tamper-proof, non-repudiable proof 
without either person needing to know anything about crypto.

## Problem
Two people agree on a price or terms verbally (a freelance job, a repair, 
a service). Later, one side disputes what was agreed. There's no reliable 
record either party can point to.

## Solution
ProofVault lets two people type up terms, both tap "I Agree," and the 
agreement (plus timestamp) gets written to Monad. A record neither party 
can quietly alter later. No wallets, no seed phrases, no crypto knowledge needed.
